Flint is a note-taking app that helps you capture ideas, connect them together, and make them part of how you think.

Try Flint See how it works

You capture and link. Then what?

Most apps optimize for getting thoughts out of your head. That's the easy part. But then your notes pile up and you never look at them again. They fail to accomplish the most important goal: changing how you think.

The hard part is making ideas actually yours: integrating them into your mental models, retrieving them at the right moment, applying them automatically.

Deep knowledge requires writing, reflection, connection-making, and active recall to turn information into understanding. Your notes should make you smarter over time. Flint is designed so they do.

The deep knowledge cycle

1

Externalize

Structured capture with minimal friction

  • Focus on taking notes not managing files and folders.
  • Note types encode different thinking patterns (daily, evergreen, predictions)
  • Daily notes encourage easy capture of ideas through daily routines.

Outcome: Ideas are captured as they come and are never lost.

2

Internalize

Routines, agent partnership, connections made visible

  • Routines systematize recurring reflection (daily reviews, weekly syntheses)
  • Agent understands note types and relationships, providing type-aware assistance
  • Links and backlinks make connections explicit and discoverable

Outcome: Notes become more valuable over time.

3

Resurface

Spaced repetition, deep re-engagement

  • Review system brings notes back at the optimal time based on recall performance
  • Agent generates contextual prompts for deep re-engagement
  • Backlinks surface where ideas recur, encouraging pattern recognition

Outcome: Your knowledge deepens over time.

# Daily Notes

Daily notes provide a frictionless way to externalize your thoughts. Each day gets its own note where you can capture ideas, reflections, and observations as they happenâ€”without worrying about where they belong or how to organize them.

## What are Daily Notes?

**Daily notes are your default space for thinking out loud.** They anchor your thoughts to time, creating a chronological record that makes it easy to:

- Capture fleeting ideas before they're lost
- Track what you're working on and learning
- Connect daily observations to larger projects through wikilinks
- Review patterns in your thinking over time

The week-based view shows seven days at once, making it natural to see context and rhythm in your work.

## Accessing Daily Notes

### Daily View

The **Daily View** shows an entire week of daily notes at once, making it easy to see your week at a glance and navigate between days.

**Access Daily View:**

1. Click "Daily" in the workspace bar at the top
2. View opens showing current week (Monday through Sunday)

**What you see:**

- Week navigation controls (Previous/Next/Today)
- Seven days displayed vertically (Mon-Sun)
- Each day shows its editor and activity

## Daily View Interface

### Week Navigation

**Navigation controls:**

- **Previous/Next buttons** - Navigate by week
- **Today button** - Jump to current week
- **Keyboard shortcuts:**
  - `T` - Jump to today
  - `[` - Previous week
  - `]` - Next week
  - `Escape` - Blur focused editor

### Daily notes

**Creating daily notes:**

- Daily notes are created automatically when you start typing
- Empty daily notes are not saved to disk
- Notes appear with minimal frontmatter: date and type

**Opening notes:**

- Click day label to open that note in the main editor
- Shift+click day label to add to notes shelf

## Browsing Historical Daily Notes

### Week View Navigation

**Navigate through time:**

- Use Previous/Next buttons to browse past weeks
- Click "Today" to jump back to current week
- Use `[` and `]` keyboard shortcuts for quick navigation
- Scroll within the week to see all seven days

### Finding Past Entries

**Via AI:**

```
You: What did I work on last Monday?

AI: [Opens daily note from last Monday]

    Last Monday (Jan 8):

    - Planned week's priorities
    - Team standup
    - Worked on API integration
    - Client meeting about timeline
    - Made good progress, identified blocker
```

**Browse activity:**

Each day in the week view shows notes you created or modified, making it easy to see what you worked on.

### Creating Summaries

**Monthly summary with AI:**

```
You: Summarize my daily notes from January into a new note

AI: [Reads all January daily notes, creates summary]

    Created: summaries/january-2025-summary.md

    January 2025 Summary:

    Projects worked on:
    - Project Alpha (primary focus)
    - Client Website
    - Documentation

    Key accomplishments:

    - 12 client meetings

```
